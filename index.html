FAQ

Common questions.

Do you work white-label under NDA?
Yes. We operate as your local Spain team. Projects are not published, referenced or disclosed. We sign NDA on request.
What is your minimum notice for mobilisation?
48h for standard retail installs. For stand builds we need at least 5 working days depending on scope. We reply within 24h to confirm feasibility.
Can you manage print and production?
Yes when needed — we coordinate with fabricators and do a pre-check on readiness before install day.
What exactly is the deliverable?
Before/after photos by area or store, an issues log with resolutions, and a sign-off summary. All delivered within 24–48h of close-out.
Do you cover Portugal?
Yes, depending on scope and timing. Include Portugal in your brief and we'll confirm feasibility alongside Spain.
How do you quote?
Send us the brief — city, scope, dates and asset list. We come back within 24h with a rough budget. No commitment needed to get a number.
